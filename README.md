# IT Support Portfolio

This is a portfolio of my practical IT support work, projects, troubleshooting notes, and labs.

I made this to show the kind of hands-on IT work I can do, especially around Windows devices, hardware, networking, Microsoft 365, Active Directory, and general user support.

The aim is to document things properly rather than just say I know them. A lot of the work here is based on the kind of issues that come up in real IT support, such as setting up devices, fixing Windows problems, helping with Microsoft 365, troubleshooting hardware, and working through problems step by step.

## About Me

I'm an entry-level IT support candidate with a T-Level in Digital Support Services, which I achieved at Merit.

I have also completed a Level 4 Cyber Security qualification and am currently waiting for my final grade.

Most of my practical experience comes from working with Windows devices, hardware, deployment, troubleshooting, and basic networking. During my work placement, I helped prepare and deploy computers for end users, including installing operating systems, checking hardware, wiping drives, and getting devices ready to be used again.

I am mainly looking to build my experience in IT support, desktop support, service desk, and general enterprise IT. I enjoy practical troubleshooting, especially when it involves figuring out why something is not working and getting it back to a usable state.
